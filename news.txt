1. US-Iran Peace Agreement:
- The United States and Iran have reached an initial agreement to end hostilities and extend a ceasefire.
- The deal aims to reopen the Strait of Hormuz and end the U.S. naval blockade of Iranian ports.
- It includes a 60-day period for negotiations on Iran's nuclear program, sanctions, and enriched uranium stockpile.
- The agreement was electronically signed on June 14, with a formal signing ceremony scheduled for June 19 in Switzerland.
- The announcement has caused reactions, including anger in Israel as the deal does not cover the withdrawal of Israeli forces from positions in Lebanon. President Trump warned that military strikes could resume if a final accord is not reached.

- Airstrikes caused damage to Kyiv's Dormition Cathedral, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
